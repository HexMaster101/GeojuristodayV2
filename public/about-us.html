    Geojuristoday

    A Youth-Lead Think Tank

    Geojuristoday is a dynamic and youth-led independent think tank with a passionate team of 30 members, including alumni and students from India's leading universities. Our mission is to contribute to the revival and growth of Bharat, while championing "The India Way" foreign policy.

    At Geojuristoday, we firmly believe in the power of international relations and diplomacy in shaping domestic lives. We offer a unique perspective on global affairs, rooted in our rich Indian heritage. Our approach is holistic, emphasizing national security and humanitarian concerns alike.

    Our commitment extends beyond discourse; we actively work towards achieving Sustainable Development Goals, reducing global carbon emissions, and mitigating greenhouse gases through informative content. We strive to foster global peace by promoting dialogue and diplomacy, aiming to reduce conflicts on a global scale.

    Join us on our journey to make India great again and create a more peaceful, sustainable, and harmonious world.
